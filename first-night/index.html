First Night

a ribcage thrums
deafening—

echos of
a shattered body

relenting midnight—
this is not the world

don't panic
the steel toe boots

will carry off
oil-slicked waters

this is not the worlddon't panic

and the death rattles
escaping

opulent mouths
petechial babes

this is not the cascading,
militant world

burial shrouds
roiling shadows

undulating red expanse
of desert sands—

the murmurs—
the rumored village

the hunger—the curled fingers
of children

burning
like tires

the same street
the same gaunt face

the same black smoke—
the billowing

unfurls toward
a forgotten—

this is not the—black and skyward

swathes of lingerers, shades, and
wastes
